# How to Load .fit Workouts onto a Wahoo Device

## Steps

1. **Open the Wahoo ELEMNT app** on your phone and make sure your Wahoo device is paired.
2. In the app, go to **Workout Profiles** → **Planned Workouts**.
3. Tap **Import Workout** and select the `.fit` file from your phone's storage.
4. The workout will sync automatically to your paired Wahoo device.
5. On your Wahoo device, go to **Workouts** to find and start the uploaded workout.

## Alternative: USB Transfer

1. **Connect your Wahoo device** to your computer using a USB cable.
2. The device will appear as a removable drive.
3. Copy your `.fit` workout file into the root folder of the device.
4. **Safely eject** the device and disconnect the cable.
5. The workout should appear under **Workouts** on the device.

## Notes

- The Wahoo ELEMNT app (iOS/Android) is the easiest way to transfer workouts.
- Make sure the `.fit` file is a valid workout file (not an activity file).
- If the workout does not appear, try restarting the device or re-syncing via the app.
