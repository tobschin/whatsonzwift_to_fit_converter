# How to Load .fit Workouts onto a Garmin Edge 540

## Steps

1. **Connect your Garmin Edge 540** to your computer using a USB-C cable.
2. The device will appear as a removable drive (e.g. `GARMIN`).
3. Navigate to the folder: `GARMIN` → `NewFiles`
4. **Copy your `.fit` workout file(s)** into the `NewFiles` folder.
5. **Safely eject** the Garmin device and disconnect the cable.
6. On your Garmin Edge 540, go to: **Training** → **Workouts** → **Cycling**
7. Your uploaded workout will appear in the list and is ready to use.

## Notes

- The Garmin will automatically move the file from `NewFiles` to the correct internal folder after the next startup.
- Make sure the `.fit` file is a valid workout file (not an activity file).
- If the workout does not appear, try restarting the device.
